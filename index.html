What is KIMCHI? 김치란 무엇인가?

Kimchi is an essential side dish in Korean cuisine.

During the fermentation process, the vegetables become slightly softer, while keeping their crunchiness. They also acquire a nice acidity offering a pleasant freshness in the mouth.
